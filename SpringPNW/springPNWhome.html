WHAT IS PIKE PLACE MARKET?

Pike Place Market is a market in downtown Seattle. It is an iconic Seattle tourist attraction where you can find food, fish, art, and a wide range of events. It is also one of the largest continuosly operating market in the US. Click the links above to explore the different aspect of Pike Place Market.
